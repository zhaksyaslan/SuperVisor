# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SuperVisor ("we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the SuperVisor plugin ("Service").

## 1. Information We Collect

Depending on your configuration, we may process:

- Account and workspace identifiers
- Service metadata (service names, environment tags, endpoint paths)
- Monitoring data (uptime checks, latency, error rates, queue signals)
- Alert delivery metadata (channel type, destination identifiers, delivery status)
- Operational logs needed for diagnostics and security

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data unless you include it in your own monitoring payloads.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide monitoring, alerting, and incident orchestration features
- Detect incidents and trigger configured automations
- Improve reliability, performance, and security
- Provide support and troubleshoot issues
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

Where required by law, we process data based on:

- Contract performance
- Legitimate interests (service security, fraud prevention, product improvement)
- Consent (where specifically requested)
- Legal obligations

## 4. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with:

- Infrastructure and hosting providers
- Notification/integration providers you enable (e.g., Slack, Telegram, webhook destinations)
- Professional advisors and legal authorities where required

We do not sell your personal data.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as needed for operational, contractual, and legal purposes. Retention periods depend on data type and your plan settings.

## 6. Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7. International Transfers

If data is transferred across borders, we apply appropriate safeguards as required by applicable law.

## 8. Your Rights

Subject to local law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, or export your data.

To submit a request, contact: **privacy@supervisor-plugin.com**

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by local law), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 11. Contact

For privacy questions, contact: **privacy@supervisor-plugin.com**
