# Privacy Policy

SuperVisor plugin does not intentionally collect personal data.

## Data Handling

- The plugin connects directly from your JetBrains IDE to your configured Supervisor XML-RPC endpoint(s).
- Process lists, statuses, and logs displayed in the plugin come from your own Supervisor instance(s).
- Connection metadata is stored locally in the IDE settings storage.
- Credentials are stored locally using JetBrains Password Safe.

## Third-Party Services

SuperVisor does not require external analytics or tracking services to operate.
If external services are added in future versions, this policy will be updated.
